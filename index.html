About us

Who we are?

Aquafleece Solutions is one of the leading building technology service firm in Nigeria an electrical and mechanical company domiciled in Nigeria. We specialize in design, installation and supply of electrical and mechanical products. Our services also include the supply of fire protection equipment and security products. We also offer consulting and after sales services to customers. We provide these services to resellers, installers and end users.
