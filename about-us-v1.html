About us

Who We Are?

Aquafleece Solutions is one of the leading building technology service firm in Nigeria an electrical and mechanical company domiciled in Nigeria. We specialize in design, installation and supply of electrical and mechanical products. Our services also include the supply of fire protection equipment and security products. We also offer consulting and after sales services to customers. We provide these services to resellers, installers and end users.

What we do?

Aquafleece Solutions specializes in supplying electrical, fire protection and security products. Its main goal is to provide customers with the best quality products and with its team of experienced and passionate professionals; the sky is the starting point in achieving these objectives.

Our services range has been categorized into different groups which represent the core of our business function and value proposition to the market.

  • Smart Home Gadgets, Electrical, & Fire Solutions
  • After-sales Customer Support Services.
  • Expert security and safety consultancy.

How we do it?

Our objective at Aquafleece Solutions is to become a household name for electrical and mechanical products, design and implementation. Having decided to put the customer at the forefront of any our operations, we seek to distinguish ourselves from competition by creating a long lasting impression and relationship with anybody we do business with and by providing customers with high quality and safe technologies and products that meets the most rigorous standards. With a history of being in the electric and power sector, we improve operational efficiency by employing integrated approaches to solve customers' complex issues such as the installation and servicing of building services installation and products.

In providing after sales supports, we use the most effective and modern technologies to respond to customers gadget problems.

We also provide advisory services to customers and fellow professionals alike by using knowledge from competitive research developed by our team of professionals that meet future and current demands.
